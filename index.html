I consider myself a scientist, educator, community builder, and translator. I am a post-doctoral researcher in Dr. Titus Brown's lab at The University of California, Davis working on community engagement for the Data Commons Pilot Phase Consortium. I'm collaborating with Dr. Rebecca Calisi on a digital video series called I Can Science that highlights diversity in innovative research and teaching. I'm also a volunteer instructor trainer, lesson developer, and Spanish translator for The Carpentries, a non-profit community-driven organization focused on teaching coding and data science skills. I have a PhD in Cell and Molecular Biology from The University of Texas at Austin. For more info, click thinks in the navigation bar above.
